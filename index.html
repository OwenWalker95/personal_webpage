About Me

I have been programming since early 2023 (If you don't count tinkering with Runescape bots in the early 2010s!). My entry to this was through game development, dabbling with C# through using the Unity game engine, but I soon realised that programming was the element of game development I enjoyed and was interested in the most so decided to learn it for it's own sake.

In February 2023 I began a computing degree course part time, whilst working as a Landscape Gardener. By July 2023 I was fortunate enough to land my first software job, leaving behind a 10 year career in manual labour to join a wholesale company as a trainee programmer. My day job consists of maintaining and developing their ERP system, written in COBOL and running on a UNIX system, with the oldest code dating to the late 70s. I also administer the system, providing support to users, assist the business with reporting from the database, coordinate 3rd party development and do acceptance testing of 3rd party solutions.

Outside of work and my university studies, I have explored other languages, spending the most time over the last 2 years learning C. After leaving numerous unfinished projects in various stages of completion, I actually finished something which was a simple Tetris clone. Along the way I have also built up a small personal C library which i've called ow_units, containing some basic functionality. Admittedly I have spent much time reinventing wheels, but that was really the point, to learn and practice.

After finishing the Tetris project and thinking 'what next?' I decided I needed to learn Git so made my Github profile, and created my first two repos, for Tetris and ow_units. Now I've turned my attention to learning the fundamentals of web development, something I will cover in my degree before long, but wanted to get a headstart. This brings me to creating this page.
